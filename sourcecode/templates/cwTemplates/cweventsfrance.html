France

AKA Francy pants and big blue blob. France is a starting major power.

France has many events as it was really important in European politics.

The Surrender of Maine

Is triggered only by England refusing to cede Maine in their event.

Two event paths can be chosen

1# "Time to teach them how to war." England declares war on France using the restoration of union casus belli. You Occupy the province of Maine.

2# "Let us back down." Lose 10 prestige, England and France sign a mutual truce. If Provence exists they gain the opinion modifier "Gave up on maine." its a -70 opinion modifier with a yearly decay of +3. If The Cossacks is active then nobility also loses 10 loyalty.

The French Revolution: Republican Monarchy

Trigger conditions are that France is a republic or its capital is controlled by rebels. Is not a revolutionary republic. Is not a revolutionary empire. The revolution target does not exist. Either a revolution has happened in the world or the art of war dlc is enabled.

"A Revolution has struck us!" France will be the revolution target. Changes government to revolutionary republic. Current ruler dies, all revolutionaries in france are disbanded. If France has a stability of less than +3 then it gains 1 stability else it gains 30 admin.
