Ming

Starting Asian/World major power

Ming have many events related to their mandate of heavan..

Repairing the Great Wall

Trigger conditions are that is Ming, year is 1450 or later but is before 1490.

Two event paths can be chosen

1# "Yes, we need to repair the Great Wall!" Lose ducats equal to 0.5 years of income.

2# "Let it continue to crumble" Lose 10 prestige.

The Perfection of Ming Porcelain

Trigger conditions you are Ming, year is 1500 or later but before 1550..

"Perfect! Every owned province that produces Chinaware in Asia gets the modifier perfect_porcelain forever.".
