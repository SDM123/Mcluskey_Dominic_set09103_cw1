Scotland

Starting European minor power

Scotland has many events related to the English incursions into Scotland.

The Auld Alliance

Trigger conditions are that the event has not fired before. Country is Scotland, England exists and France exists, dont be at war with France. Year is between 1512 and 1550.

Two event paths can be chosen

1# "Good relations with France" France gets opinion modifier Good relations towards Scotland which gives +50 opinion. And Scotland gets the same towards France. England gets an irritated -10 opinion modifier with Scotland.

2# "We shall have nothing to do with France!" England gets a +20 opinion modifier towards Scotland. France gets an opinion modifier of -10 towards Scotland..

Scottish Witchcraft Act

Trigger conditions are that it can fire only once. Year is between 1563 and 1735. Is not in a regency and is Scotland.

Two event paths can be chosen.

#1 "Defend the Witchcraft Act" adds ruler modifier "Scottish witchcraft act" which gives +1 papal influence per year. +5 Tech cost.

#2 "Denounce it" lose 5 prestige.
