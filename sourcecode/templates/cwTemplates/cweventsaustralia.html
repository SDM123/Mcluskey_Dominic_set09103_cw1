Australia

Formable post colonial Oceanian power

Oceania is most often neglected. Australia probably has little to no unique events so I will include the formation option.

Form Australian Nation

Trigger conditions are that was never an end-game tag. Is a colonial nation or former colonial nation. Owns a province in colonial Australia. Has not formed any reformable nations or formable colonial nations before. Australia does not exist, its not at war and is not a subject nation.

Country changes to Australia. Gain a permanent claim on all unowned Australian provinces. Gain 25 prestige. Gets modifier increased centralisation for 20 years which gives -0.05 monthly autonomy change and +1 national unrest. If it is in the HRE but not an elector or the emperor then remove all provinces from the HRE. If it is a duchy then form a kingdom.
