Comet Sighted

Can be randomly triggered at any point.

1# "It's an omen" -1 Stability.

2# "The end is nigh!" -1 Stability.

3# "Ignore the peasant rabble" -1 Stability.

4# "Stop looking at the sky!" -1 Stability.
