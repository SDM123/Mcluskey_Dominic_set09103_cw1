Mali

Starting African power

Mali has a bunch of events about their continued administration of the parts of Africa they control.

Malian Authority Under Threat

Trigger conditions are that country is Mali, stability is at least 0. Has to be before 1550, Owns province Trarza, Cayor, Wolof, Siine and Jolof doesn't exist..

Two event paths can be chosen

1# "Mali's decline ends here!" 4 Nationalist rebels size 2 rise up in every province listed above as a trigger requirement..

2# "We can make some concessions..." Release Jolof as a vassal.

Emergence of the Fulani

Trigger conditions are that province is Kantor, Fulo doesn't exist, year is 1470 or later. Tekur is empty..

Province Trarza, Bambuk, Kanto, Termes, Wagadu, Baghena all become a new nation Fulo. Fulo gains a ruler with 4 military skill and trait conqueror. Even if you have these provinces it will flip to the Fulo.
