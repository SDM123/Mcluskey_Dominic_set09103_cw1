Kongo

Starting African power

Kongo has many events related to religion as they converted to Christianity when the Portugese showed up.

Portuguese Missionaries

Trigger conditions are that you are Kongo, Portugal has religion Catholic, Kongolese Coast has discovered Portugal and Kongo isn't already Catholic.

Two event paths can be chosen

1# "Let them preach." Lose 1 stability, lose 25 prestige, lose 25 legitimacy. One random owned province flips Catholic. Sets flag kongo_allows_missionaries. Portugal gains a positive opinion modifier. Portugal gets the event "Missionaries Enter the Kongo!".

2# "Drive off the missionaries!" If this country has a stability of less than +3 then it gains 1 stability or else gains 50 admin. Gain 10 prestige and portugal gets a negative opinion modifier. Portugal gets the event "Missionaries rebuffed".

The King of Kongo Converts!

Trigger conditions are Kongo has flag kongo_allows_missionaries, religion isn't Catholic, ruler is male and any owned province has religion Catholic.

Two event paths can be chosen

#1 "Christianity will triumph in Kongo!" Changes country religion to Catholic. If government is tribal change it to despotic monarchy. Lose 3 stability. Portugal gets the event "Conversion of the Kongo!".

#2 "Off with his head!" Ruler dies.
