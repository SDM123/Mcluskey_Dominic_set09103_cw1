Huron

Starting American minor power

Huron doesn't have any specifically unique events but it does have events related to being an American navite tribe.

Frozen Lands

Trigger conditions are that Conquest of Paradise is active, government is native council, capital doesn't have arctic climate, the flag natives_adapted_arctic, a colony has at least 100 settlers and doesn't have more than 500 settlers. Month must be October and not May.

Two event paths can be chosen

1# "Our way of life is to adapt. Send others to teach them." Gains you 200 population in colony, country loses 1 stability and set the flag "natives_ adapted_arctic

2# "Let them leave if they so wish." Lose 100 population in the colony.

Our New Home in the North

Trigger conditions are that Conquest of Paradise is active. Government is native council. Doesn't own at least 2 non-colony provinces, Doesn't have at least 1 colony, neighbouring country has capital with arctic climate. Doesn't have flag natives_adapted_arctic. Month is October and isn't May.

2 event paths to choose.

#1 "In our past home, we were wise, but here, they are wiser." Gain modifier "Pleased" towards our country. Set flag natives_adapted_arctic and lose 10 prestige.

#2 "We learn better from the land itself." Gain modifier "Learning from the land" for a year. Lose 2000 manpower and gain flag natives_adapted_arctic.
