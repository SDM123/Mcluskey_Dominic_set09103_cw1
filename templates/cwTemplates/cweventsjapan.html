Japan

Formable Asian major power

Japan have a lot of events about Japanese feudal life, and new developments of culture that happened during the perior of time Eu4 is set in.

The Sakoku Law

Trigger conditions is Japan has the modifier "Sakoku Law", is not a subject nation, has had the flag Sakoku Law for at least 7300 days. Doesn't have the flag jap_sakoku, doesn't have modifier "Follow Sakoku" or modifier "Question Sakoku".

Two event paths can be chosen

1# "Keep following the Sakoku law." Gain modifier "Follow Sakoku" for 10 years. Set country flag jap_sakoku.

2# "Abandon the Sakoku law." Gain modifier "Question Sakoku" for 10 years, lose 1 stability. Remove modifier "Sakoku Law" set flag jap_sakoku. All your subjects switch to the same modifiers.

Unruly Samurai

Trigger conditions are ulture is in Japanese group and is not at war.

Two event paths can be chosen

#1 "They seem useful!" Gain modifier "Unruly Samurai" for 10 years. Gain 10 army tradition.

#2 "The punishment matches the crime." Lose 10 army tradition and gain 10 prestige.
