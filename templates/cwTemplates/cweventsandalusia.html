Andalusia

Formable European power

Andalusia doesn't have unique events. It can be formed by any muslim nations with Andalusian/Maghrebi as primary culture. The formation event will be shown below.

Restore Al-Andalus

Trigger conditions are that Andalusia does not exist, is not subject nation, is not nomad nation, is not tribal and owns Granada, Sevillam Cordoba, Gibraltar, Jaen, Cadiz, Badajoz, Almeria and either (Algarve and Beja) or (Alicante and Valencia).

Effects of the event are as follows. Country changes to Andalusia, gain 25 prestige, gain a permanent claim on Toledo, Upper and Lower Andalucia, Algarve, Beja, Badajoz, Valencia, Alicante and Extremandura. If Andalusian isn't primary culture it becomes primary culture. If its part of the Holy Roman Empire but not the emperor or an elector then all its provinces are removed from the HRE.If it is a duchy then it becomes a kingdom rank and Cordoba becomes the capital.
